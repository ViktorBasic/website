Who Am I?

I'm photographer and graphic designer with high ambition. I'm a creative thinker. I like to explore alternative solutions to problems and I have an open mind about what will work best.


VIKTOR BAŠIĆ
Zagreb, Croatia
– Photography –

My eyes are what I use most. Buying the first and only camera brand Nikon has started my quest to explore what my eyes really see and how to look at it in the best possible way. Street photo is what attracted me most and for the time being. For me the biggest challenge and also the goal is to take photos of things that others do not see. Show "the other" side that people do not see!
